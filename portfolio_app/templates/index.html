Who is Emily Wivell?

In 1999, at the age of ten, I became an award winning programmer. I brought home a trophy for a computer program I wrote about Space Shuttles. Since then, coding has always held a warm place in my heart. I have taken a few classes over the years, most recently a Java class in the winter of 2015, and coded in my spare time as a hobby. I would open up notepad and type up html some html while at work.

It wasn't until I found THE IRON YARD GREENVILLE that I challenged myself to turn my hobby into a career. Aside for coding I also enjoy a good pun and I like to show off my amazing coding skills. I absolutely LOVE to learn new things which means my days are full and asking questions and finding answers. Pretty things are good, but pretty things with functionality are the best!
